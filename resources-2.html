Mental Health Matters

Mental health is a state of mental well-being that enables people to cope with the stresses of life, realize their abilities, learn well and work well, and contribute to their community.

1

What is stress?

Stress is how we react when we feel under pressure or threatened. It usually happens when we are in a situation that we don't feel we can manage or control.

2

Causes of stress

Stress can be caused through many different ways such as feeling under a lot of pressure, facing big changes within your life or when you dont have any control over a situation, these situations can be easily avoided by taking a break from reality and taking some time for yourself. A situation can become alot easier from just taking a break.

3

How to deal with pressure?

Dealing with pressure is another factor of stress, there are various steps you can take such as, identifying your triggers, organise your time, address some of the causes, and accept the things you cant change.

4

Managing stress

Being prepared for periods of stress can make it easier to get through them. And knowing how to manage our wellbeing can help us recover after a stressful event. Some of us may refer to our ability to manage stress as our resilience.

5

Treatment of stress

There is no physical treatment for stress, however, there are some things you can do to help, such as meditate, speaking to a GP or a therapist, and dissconecting from your situation.

6

Can stress cause illness?

Stress can absolutly cause illness, high stress levels can lead high blood pressure. Don't let a situation you can't control negativly effect your mental and physical health.
